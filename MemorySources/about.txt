About Solliance:
Solliance was founded in 1998 to give lone wolf, technical experts a pack to run with. 
There are over 300 experts in Solliance.
The experts are located all over the world, across 18 countries.
Solliance has more than 29 Microsoft MVP's.
Our MVP's have award categories that include Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Azure cloud, Developer Tools and Security.
The leadership of Solliance includes Zoiner Tejada (CEO) , Michele Leroux Bustamante (President), Brian Noyes (CTO) and Jenny Madsen (COO).
Solliance has nine practices, each representing specialist experts.
